Atomic Wallet Membership

Introduction

Atomic is the first wallet started a cashback based on its native token called AWC. All customers holding a minimum of 1.000 AWC will receive a cashback for using a built-in exchange service. For each exchange operation, you will receive a reward depending on the amount of AWC you hold.

Cashback levels

Atomic Wallet introduces you to 4 cashback levels. Start from a Bronze and move to the Platinum by holding more AWC on your balance. Each level will increase your exchange cashback and will bring you more benefits.


Why Cashback?

The programme itself incentivize new users to become AWC holders and to gain mass adoption on the cryptocurrency wallet market.

How to Participate

  1. Deposit AWC to Atomic

    Minimum requirement is 1,000 AWC.

  2. Start an Exchange

    Your cashback will be counted based on your BEP2-AWC balance.

  3. Receive AWC

    Rewards will be distributed on a monthly basis.
    Hold more AWC to increase your profit.
